# Handcrafted Haven

## Overview

Handcrafted Haven is a modern and community-driven digital marketplace designed to empower artisans, crafters, and independent creators by providing them with an online platform to showcase and sell their handmade products.

The platform bridges the gap between skilled artisans and conscious consumers who appreciate originality, sustainability, and handcrafted excellence. Unlike mass-produced marketplaces, Handcrafted Haven focuses on authentic craftsmanship, cultural creativity, and personalized shopping experiences.

The application aims to create a supportive ecosystem where artisans can grow their businesses while customers can discover meaningful products with unique stories behind them.

# Problem Statement

Many talented artisans struggle to reach broader audiences due to limited access to digital marketplaces, expensive platform fees, and lack of technical resources.

Customers who value authentic handmade products often face challenges finding trustworthy platforms dedicated specifically to artisans and handcrafted goods.

Handcrafted Haven addresses these challenges by creating a centralized and user-friendly marketplace tailored specifically for handmade commerce.
