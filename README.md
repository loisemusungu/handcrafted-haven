# Handcrafted Haven

## Overview

Handcrafted Haven is an innovative web application designed to provide artisans and crafters with a digital marketplace where they can showcase and sell unique handcrafted products. The platform connects talented creators with customers who value handmade craftsmanship, sustainability, and locally produced goods.

The application promotes:

* Community engagement among artisans
* Sustainable and conscious shopping
* Support for local creators and small businesses
* Discovery of authentic handmade products
